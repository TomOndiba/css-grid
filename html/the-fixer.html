The Fixer

By James Fallows
Photography by Alan

When Matthew Ive was elected Apple's Senior Vice President of Industrial Design at 36 he was seen only in contrast to his father, Jonathon, who created the iconic iPod, iPhone and iPad before him. With his father in retirement, and the recent merger of Google and Samsung, he is seen as someone who might save a company that many consider broken. His design for the world's first artificially intelligent holographic computer is brilliant, but will it be enough to restore the Apple dream?
